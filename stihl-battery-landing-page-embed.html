Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Still unsure? Explore our FAQ or visit our showroom to speak with one of our specialists.

Can I use an AK battery in an AP tool, or vice versa?

No, the AK and AP battery ranges are physically different sizes and connection configurations. AK batteries only work in tools from the AK range, and AP batteries only work in tools from the AP range. However, any battery within a specific system will fit all tools within that same system.

What is special about the new AK 30 S battery?

The STIHL AK 30 S battery is a high-output battery within the AK system. While it holds the same energy capacity (180 Wh) as the standard AK 30, it has been engineered with optimized cells to deliver higher continuous electrical current. This makes it especially suited for demanding tools like cordless chainsaws, providing stronger, sustained cutting performance.

Can I work in the rain with my cordless STIHL tools?

Only with the AP system. STIHL AP system tools and AP batteries are constructed with special weather seals and hold certified IPX4 splash protection ratings. This means they are fully tested and approved to work in wet, rainy, and damp weather. AK tools do not have this rating and should only be used in dry, clear conditions.

How long do the batteries take to charge?

Charging times depend on the combination of battery capacity and charger model. For example, using the AL 101 charger, an AK 20 battery takes about 180 minutes to reach 100%. If you upgrade to the AL 301 fast charger, an AP 300 S battery can be charged to 100% in just 55 minutes, and an AP 500 S battery takes approximately 55 minutes to reach 80% capacity.
